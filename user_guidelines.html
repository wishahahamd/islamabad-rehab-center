1. Introduction & Authentication

Welcome to the Islamabad Rehab Center (IRC) system guideline handbook. This manual provides clear instructions for system administrators, clinicians, and patients on navigating the portal.

The portal is designed around a secure Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) model. Access to pages, database actions, and navigation components are resolved dynamically based on your login profile.

1.1 Log in to the System

To begin using the application, navigate to the local portal link and log in:

  1. Step 1 Open your web browser and navigate to: http://localhost:8000.
  2. Step 2 Click the Portal Login button on the navbar or select your portal card.
  3. Step 3 Input your registered Email and Password.
  4. Step 4 Click Sign In. The system will automatically direct you to your specific dashboard workspace.

1.2 Dark Mode Theme Toggle

The system includes a local theme configuration that persists across sessions. To switch between Light Mode and Dark Mode:

  • On the login page: Click the Toggle Theme button at the bottom of the card.
  • Inside the portal: Click the Sun/Moon icon on the top right header bar.

2. Administrative Portal Guidelines

The Administrative Portal is restricted to Super Admins and contains configuration tools to manage the backend skeleton.

2.1 Managing Users & Credentials

  1. Step 1 Navigate to System Management ➔ Manage Users.
  2. Step 2 Use the Add User form to register new doctors, counselors, or patients.
  3. Step 3 To deactivate or reactivate an account, toggle the status switch in the user registry grid.

2.2 Dynamic Pages and Sidebar Mappings

  1. Step 1 Navigate to System Management ➔ Manage Pages.
  2. Step 2 Register new pages by specifying the script URL, parent category, and icon class.
  3. Step 3 Configure role permissions by checking the access boxes next to each role header.

2.3 System Branding Panel

  1. Step 1 On the main Admin Dashboard, locate the Center Branding Settings widget.
  2. Step 2 Edit the Rehab Center Name or the Footer Attribution Text.
  3. Step 3 Click Update Brand Details to apply branding updates across the public landing page and portals.

3. Clinician & Doctor Portal Guidelines

The Clinician Portal provides medical tools for Doctors, Psychologists, Case Counselors, and Clinical Directors to manage recoveries.

3.1 Admitting a Patient (Intake Process)

  1. Step 1 Navigate to Clinical Operations ➔ Patient Intake.
  2. Step 2 Complete the New Patient Intake form, inputting the patient's full name, age, gender, and admission date.
  3. Step 3 Select the initial treatment stage (Intake, Detox, Rehab, Outpatient, Discharged).
  4. Step 4 Assign a medical therapist and document the clinical diagnosis history. Save the record.

3.2 Formulating Treatment Recovery Plans

  1. Step 1 Navigate to Clinical Operations ➔ Treatment Plans.
  2. Step 2 Select the target patient from the directory list on the left side.
  3. Step 3 Input the detox medical schedule, therapy/cognitive behavioral goals (CBT/DBT), and aftercare guidelines. Click Save Treatment Plan.

3.3 Logging Therapy Sessions

  1. Step 1 Navigate to Clinical Operations ➔ Therapy Sessions.
  2. Step 2 Select the patient, conducting clinician, date, and therapy type.
  3. Step 3 Document the clinician assessment notes. Click Log Session.

3.4 Resolving Support Tickets

  1. Step 1 Navigate to Clinical Operations ➔ Support Tickets (or check the dashboard alert).
  2. Step 2 For pending tickets, write your response notes in the resolution textbox.
  3. Step 3 Click Resolve. The status changes to Resolved and is displayed on the patient's dashboard.

4. Patient Portal Guidelines

The Patient Portal is designed for recovering clients, providing transparency over their personal recovery timeline.

4.1 Monitoring Progress and Goals

  • Caseload Details: The dashboard displays the patient's ID, active admission stage, and assigned doctor.
  • Treatment Plan Goals: Patients can view the custom therapy goals and detox protocols formulated by their medical supervisor.
  • Session Timeline: Displays logged therapy dates, types (individual/group), and clinician progress reviews.

4.2 Submitting Daily Mood Logs

  1. Step 1 On the main Patient Dashboard, locate the Daily Mood Logger card.
  2. Step 2 Select the emoji that represents your mood status (Distressed, Struggling, Neutral, Good, Excellent).
  3. Step 3 Add daily reflections or withdrawal notes in the text input. Click Log Status.

4.3 Requesting Support & Assistance

  1. Step 1 Navigate to Clinical Operations ➔ Support Tickets (or use the sidebar link).
  2. Step 2 Complete the New Assistance Ticket form, detailing your subject and question.
  3. Step 3 Click Send Request. You can view responses and resolution notes in the ticket history log.
